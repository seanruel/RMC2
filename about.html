RateMyContractor

RateMyContractor was created with one goal in sight - to help people connect with the right contractor for their project. We are a growing compilation of homeowner’s experiences with local contractors. The people who join Rate My Contractor are not only interested in sharing their experience but are looking for a trustworthy service professional that will perform the high quality work that all homeowners deserve

RateMyContractor members submit more than 1,000 reports each month about the companies they’ve hired. This allows our members to be selective about who they hire in order to get the job done right – the first time.

Through our service, you will be able to easily tell thousands of member about you experience with a company. This is important since companies that do high quality work deserve to thrive - "Hire the Best, Forget the Rest"
